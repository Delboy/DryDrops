Jack Daniels - Founder of Dry Drops

Contact Us View Our Products

Dry Drops was founded in late 2018 after Jack Daniels' lifelong battle with alcohol finally came to an end. Instead of investing all his time, energy and money into his addiction he decided to invest it into a business to help others get out of the downward spiral he found himself in.

One key part to Jack's journey was alcohol free beverages. Not wanting to miss out on all the fantastic flavours one comes accustomed to when drinking, and finding that sometimes you just need that little something to fill the void but without the added guilt, zero percent alcohol seemed like a no brainer! However, Jack noticed a notable gap in this market when trying to shop for his favourite drinks that were also suitable for people practising sobriety. Seeing this as an opportunity to not only help himself but to help others, Jack, with nothing to lose, jumped in feet first and as a result this website was born!

'Now and again you just need something to scratch the itch without undoing all the hard work you've achieved' - Jack Daniels.

Shortly after conceiving Dry Drops, Jack came to realise that Alcohol free drinks was not just limited to people who struggle with Alcohol Abuse, but could also be extended to and not limited by teetotalers, designated drivers, pregnant women, religious practitioners and anybody who just wants to enjoy the many flavours found in alcohol but does not want that ever impending dreaded hangover! The idea was set, the market was ripe and Dry Drops was ready to step up to the plate.

'A problem shared is a problem halved' - Jack Daniels

Like everything in life, it's always easier with a little help. Building Dry Drops was no different. We here are a team of many very passionate people who all believe in the same goal, Help people to quit drinking. To partner that sentiment a percentage of all our profits go to registered charities all devoted to helping people like Jack who struggled for years with alcoholism.

All of us here hope you enjoy what we've built and thank you very much for considering our services.
